# Credits — teacher assets

## Default avatar — `teacher/avatar.glb`
**"RobotExpressive" by Quaternius (Tomás Laulhé) — CC0 (public domain)**, distributed via
the three.js examples (rig/animation tweaks by Don McCurdy). Free to use; CC0 requires no
attribution, credited here anyway.

To use your own face instead: drop a glTF/GLB at `teacher/avatar.glb`, or open the page
with `?avatar=<url>`. A VRoid or Ready-Player-Me export works; a per-model camera tweak
(the `camera.position.set(...)` line in `avatar.html`) may be wanted.
